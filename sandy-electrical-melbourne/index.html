  What We Do

Our Services

Complete electrical services for your home, office, or worksite — from simple repairs to full installations.

Popular

Electrical Installations

From new home wiring to full commercial fit-outs, we handle all electrical installations with precision and safety. Upgraded switchboards, sub-mains, and dedicated circuits for your appliances.

Lighting Design & Install

Transform your space with custom lighting solutions. We install downlights, pendants, track lighting, outdoor security lights, and smart home LED systems for efficiency and ambience.

Electrical Repairs & Safety

Tripping breakers, flickering lights, or faulty outlets — we diagnose and fix electrical issues fast. We also install safety switches (RCDs) and surge protection for peace of mind.

Power Points & Data Cabling

Need extra power points or structured data cabling? We install GPOs, USB outlets, Cat6 data points, and TV antenna outlets for modern homes and offices.

Industrial Electrical

Specialised industrial electrical contracting for factories, warehouses, and commercial facilities. Equipment supply, motor installations, three-phase power, and compliance testing.

Safety Inspections & Certificates

Full Electrical Safety Inspections for property sales, rentals, or peace of mind. We issue compliance certificates and identify any hazardous wiring or non-compliant installations.
