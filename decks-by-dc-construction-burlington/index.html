  Materials

We Build With Premium Materials

The right material makes the difference between a deck that lasts 10 years and one that lasts 30. We help you choose what fits your home, your budget, and Vermont's climate.

Pressure-Treated Lumber

The proven workhorse for Vermont decks. Rot and insect resistant, handles freeze-thaw reliably, and takes stain beautifully.

Composite Decking

Low-maintenance, splinter-free, and available in a range of colors. All the look of wood — none of the annual upkeep.

Cedar

Naturally rot-resistant and beautiful. Cedar ages gracefully in Vermont's climate — our go-to for gazebos and decorative trim.

Stainless Hardware

We use stainless or hot-dipped galvanized fasteners throughout — rust-proof in Vermont conditions, tight for decades.
