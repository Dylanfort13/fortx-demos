  What We Do

Our Services

From custom furniture to full commercial fitouts — we design, fabricate, and install with precision.

Custom Furniture

Bespoke furniture designed and handcrafted for your space — desks, cabinets, shelving, and storage solutions built to your exact specifications.

Shop & Salon Fitouts

Complete commercial fitouts including reception desks, treatment stations, cabinetry, and kitchenettes — delivered on time and on brand.

Indoor & Outdoor Signage

Custom-cut signage in aluminium, birch ply, and more. CNC precision for vehicle signage, office branding, and architectural features.

CNC Cutting & Routing

Precision CNC routing in birch plywood, aluminium, and more. Perfect for custom components, decorative panels, and architectural details.

Built-in Cabinetry

Custom walk-in robes, built-in storage, and room dividers. Tailored storage solutions that maximise your space beautifully.

Stone Top Installation

Professional stone benchtop supply and installation for kitchens, bathrooms, and commercial spaces. Premium finishes, fitted perfectly.
